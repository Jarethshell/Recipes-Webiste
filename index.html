Creamy Garlic Pasta

A rich and comforting pasta made with butter, garlic, cream, and parmesan cheese. Perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter. Add minced garlic and cook for 30‚Äì45 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Pour in cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in parmesan, black pepper, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Add cooked pasta and toss until evenly coated. If sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water 1 tablespoon at a time.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.

Classic Vegetable Stir-Fry

‚è± 25 mins ‚Ä¢ üçΩ 3 servings ‚Ä¢ ‚≠ê Beginner

A colorful and healthy stir-fry loaded with crisp vegetables and a savory homemade sauce. Great with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated

For the sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp honey or brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok over high heat. Add garlic and ginger; stir for 20 seconds.
  2. Add all vegetables and stir-fry for 4‚Äì5 minutes until bright and slightly tender but still crisp.
  3. In a bowl, whisk sauce ingredients until smooth.
  4. Pour sauce into wok and toss continuously for 1‚Äì2 minutes until vegetables are coated and sauce thickens.
  5. Remove from heat, top with spring onions, and serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and no dry flour remains. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  3. Microwave on high for 60‚Äì90 seconds (time depends on microwave power). Cake should rise and look set on top.
  4. Rest for 1 minute before eating. Enjoy warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
